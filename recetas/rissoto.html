Butternut squash rissoto

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of rice
  • 4-5 cups of of chicken stocl
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 small butternut squash
  • 1 tb spoon of butter
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Chop the "neck" of the butternut squash in small cubes and cook them in the oven until they are soft on the inside and crispy in the outside
  2. Roast the "head" of the butternut squast until its soft, then use a blender to make a butternut squash puree, and add a lid of stock to it. Salt to taste
  3. Chops the onions in small dices and cook along with the garlic in olive oil until they are translucent
  4. Add the rice and cook unntil its toasted and becomes to look translucent
  5. Add the wine and stir ocassionally until it absorbs all the liquid
  6. Add the butternut squash puree and stir until it absorbs
  7. Keep adding stock one lid at a time until the rice its cooked and has gained a creamy consistency. Salt to taste throughout the cooking process
  8. Remove from the heat and add the butter and cheese. If necessary, also add a bit stock to adjust the consistency
  9. Plate with a portion of the roasted butternut squash cubes
